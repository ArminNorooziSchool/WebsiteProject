What is Pokemon?

Pokemon is a Japanese Video Game and Anime Franchise that first released in 1996 with the game Red and Green in Japan, and Red and Blue internationally. Later in 1997 a Pokemon Anime began to broadcast amassing a large viewership very quickly.

What does Pokemon mean?

Pokemon's full name is Pocket-Monsters, and Pokemon is supposed to be a simplified name.

How do the games work?

Currently there are 8 main-line generations of Pokemon, starting with Red and Blue, and with Scarlet and Violet being the most recent released in 2022. The games follow a format where you are given a starter, and go around the main story catching more pokemon, beating trainers, gyms and evil teams. Eventually challenging the Elite 4 and Champion, in which you will defeat and become the new champion of the region. Every new region adds more new Pokemon, a new story, and new mechanics.

How does combat work?

In the Pokemon games every pokemon has up to 2 typings, these typings dictate what moves are super effective on them, and what moves are ineffective. Every pokemon has 4 moves, each also having a typing, power level, accuracy, and a counter of how many times it may be used. When a move is used the amount of damage done is based on the moves power, and the type of pokemon it is being used on, if you use a fire move against a Gyrados it will be not very effective and do half the damage because of Gyrados being a water and flying type, but if an electric type move is used on a Gyrados it is super effective and will do double the damage.

What are the Pokemon Typings?

There are multiple types like water, fire, grass, and electric and they all have different effects on one another. The most important type trio is that Water beats Fire, Fire beats Grass, and Grass beats Water.

Who is the mascot of Pokemon?

The mascot has been Pikachu due to the anime. Within the anime, the main character is Ash Ketchum, whos primary pokemon and best friend is a Pikachu. Pikachu is a electric pokemon and resembles a mouse. Pikachu is also a very good choice for a mascot as it is a very cute, friendly, and loveable pokemon, allowing both younger and older audiences to love Pikachu.
